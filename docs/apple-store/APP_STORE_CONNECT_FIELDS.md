## App Review Information — notes (suggested)

- A **reviewer test account** is required (credentials provided in App Store Connect; do not commit real logins in the repository).
- **Location** is used only while the app is in use for start/stop shift and job-site verification (not for unrelated tracking).
- **Face ID** / biometric sign-in is optional where supported; users can use password-based login if applicable.
- The app does not use third-party **advertising** or ad SDKs; **no tracking** for advertising purposes as described in the privacy label.

Adjust wording in App Store Connect to match the exact build and your privacy answers.
